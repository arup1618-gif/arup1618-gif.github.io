Beyond Mathematics

Outside of my academic work, I spend my time exploring a few other interests.

Cooking

I started cooking out of necessity when I moved into my own place during my university days. What began as simply making traditional daily meals eventually turned into a genuine interest, especially when experimenting with new recipes alongside friends. Today, I view cooking as a holistic process. I enjoy the entire routine—from sourcing fresh ingredients and careful preparation to maintaining a clean, highly efficient workspace while cooking.

Music

I wanted to learn the flute since my time in boarding school, but the opportunity did not arise until university. After mentioning this interest to a professor, they kindly connected me with a master, allowing me to formally study Indian classical music for two years. Although the pandemic abruptly interrupted my training—I traveled home thinking it was a short break and left my instruments behind—I am now gradually getting back into the rhythm of practicing and self-learning.
